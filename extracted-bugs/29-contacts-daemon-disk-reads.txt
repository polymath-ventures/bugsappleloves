# Contacts Daemon Excessive Disk Activity

## Mentions: 1 commenter
## Severity: LOW-MEDIUM

## Affected Products
- macOS Contacts

## Duration
- Ongoing

## Description
The contactsd daemon consumes massive amounts of disk I/O:
- Hundreds of gigabytes of disk reads
- Causes system slowdown
- Drains laptop battery
- No apparent reason for the activity

## User Reports
- "Activity Monitor shows contactsd reading 500GB from disk"

## Workarounds
- Kill the process (temporary, it restarts)
- Rebuild contacts database
- Disable contact sync

## Technical Notes
- Daemon: /System/Library/PrivateFrameworks/ContactsFoundation.framework/contactsd
- May be triggered by sync issues
- Possibly related to corrupt contacts
