# Safari Tabs Randomly Disappear

## Mentions: 1 commenter
## Severity: MEDIUM

## Affected Products
- iOS Safari

## Duration
- Ongoing

## Description
Open Safari tabs get randomly deleted:
- Tabs disappear without user action
- Tab groups vanish unexpectedly
- Happens after updates or restarts
- No way to recover lost tabs

## User Reports
- "I had 50 tabs open and Safari just deleted them all"
- "My tab groups keep disappearing"

## Workarounds
- Use Reading List as backup
- Bookmark important tabs immediately
- Use a different browser
- Accept tab impermanence

## Impact
Loss of research, reading lists, and work in progress.
