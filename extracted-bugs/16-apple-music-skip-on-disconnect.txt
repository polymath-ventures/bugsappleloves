# Apple Music Skips Repeatedly on Network Drop

## Mentions: 2 commenters
## Severity: MEDIUM

## Affected Products
- iOS Apple Music

## Duration
- 10+ years

## Description
When network connection drops briefly, Apple Music starts skipping through songs rapidly:
- One network hiccup causes multiple skips
- Songs change every few seconds
- Downloaded songs also affected
- Have to manually stop and restart playback

## User Reports
- "I go through a tunnel and Apple Music skips 10 songs"
- "This has been happening for over 10 years"
- "Even songs I downloaded offline get skipped"

## Workarounds
- Use a different music app (Spotify handles this better)
- Download all music for offline use
- Manually restart playback after network issues

## Technical Notes
- Seems to be a state machine bug in playback logic
- Network retry logic triggers skip instead of retry
