# Music Artwork Randomizes Across Syncs

## Mentions: 2 commenters
## Severity: LOW-MEDIUM

## Affected Products
- iOS Music app

## Duration
- Years

## Description
Album artwork gets mixed up and randomized when syncing music:
- Wrong album art displays for songs
- Artwork changes randomly between syncs
- Embedded artwork replaced with wrong images
- Compilation albums particularly affected

## User Reports
- "My carefully curated album art is now completely wrong"
- "Every sync scrambles my artwork more"
- "A Beatles album is showing Metallica artwork"

## Workarounds
- Re-embed artwork manually (time-consuming)
- Use a different music player
- Accept the chaos

## Technical Notes
- Related to iTunes/Music library database
- iCloud Music Library makes it worse
- May be related to Apple Music matching
