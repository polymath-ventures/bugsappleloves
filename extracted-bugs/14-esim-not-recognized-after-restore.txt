# eSIM Not Recognized After Backup Restore

## Mentions: 2 commenters
## Severity: MEDIUM

## Affected Products
- iOS

## Duration
- Years

## Description
When restoring an iPhone from a backup that came from a device with dual SIM configuration, the eSIM is not recognized:
- Phone won't acknowledge eSIM exists
- Carrier settings don't show eSIM option
- Have to contact carrier to re-provision eSIM
- Happens even when restoring to same device

## User Reports
- "After restoring my phone, my eSIM just disappeared"
- "Had to spend 2 hours with carrier support to get eSIM working again"
- "This happens every time I restore from backup"

## Workarounds
- Re-provision eSIM with carrier after restore
- Set up as new phone instead of restoring
- Use physical SIM instead

## Impact
Significant time waste dealing with carrier support to re-activate eSIM.
