# Photos Locked From Deletion

## Mentions: 1 commenter
## Severity: LOW-MEDIUM

## Affected Products
- iOS Photos

## Duration
- Ongoing

## Description
Some photos cannot be deleted from iPhone despite not being in cloud or on other devices:
- Delete option grayed out or missing
- Photo appears locked for no reason
- Not part of shared albums or cloud sync
- Takes up storage that can't be freed

## User Reports
- "I have photos I can't delete and they're not in iCloud"

## Workarounds
- Connect to computer and delete via Finder/iTunes
- Use a third-party photo manager
- Reset and restore (nuclear option)
