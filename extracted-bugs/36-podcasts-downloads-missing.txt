# Apple Podcasts Downloaded Episodes Don't Appear

## Mentions: 1 commenter
## Severity: LOW-MEDIUM

## Affected Products
- iOS Podcasts

## Duration
- Years

## Description
Episodes that have been downloaded fail to appear in the app:
- Download completes but episode not listed
- Only appears when storage reserve file is present
- Have to re-download episodes
- Storage shows space used but content missing

## User Reports
- "I downloaded episodes for my flight and they weren't there"

## Workarounds
- Re-download episodes before travel
- Use a different podcast app (Overcast, Pocket Casts)
- Check available storage
