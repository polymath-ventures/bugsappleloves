# Audio Balance Randomly Changes

## Mentions: 1 commenter
## Severity: LOW-MEDIUM

## Affected Products
- macOS

## Duration
- 6+ years documented

## Description
The left/right audio balance shifts without user action:
- Audio suddenly favors one side
- Balance setting changed in System Preferences
- Happens randomly, no trigger identified
- Particularly annoying with headphones

## User Reports
- "I've filed bug reports for 6 years about this"
- "Audio randomly becomes left-heavy"

## Workarounds
- Check and reset balance regularly
- Use audio control software
- Accept periodic mono audio experience
