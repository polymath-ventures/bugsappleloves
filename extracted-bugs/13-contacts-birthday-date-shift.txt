# Contacts Birthday Date Shift During Sync

## Mentions: 2 commenters
## Severity: MEDIUM

## Affected Products
- macOS Contacts
- iOS Contacts
- Office 365 sync

## Duration
- Ongoing

## Description
When syncing contacts with Office 365/Exchange, birthday dates shift forward by one day:
- Contact has birthday on Jan 15
- After sync, birthday shows as Jan 16
- Happens repeatedly with each sync
- Eventually birthdays are completely wrong

## User Reports
- "Every sync adds a day to my contacts' birthdays"
- "Had to stop syncing with Exchange because of this"
- "My wife's birthday is now showing as a week later than it should be"

## Workarounds
- Manually correct dates (temporary)
- Stop syncing birthdays with Exchange
- Use a different contacts app

## Technical Notes
- Likely timezone/UTC conversion bug
- Classic off-by-one date bug
- Only affects Exchange/O365 sync
