# Bluetooth Audio Stutters During Fullscreen Switch

## Mentions: 1 commenter
## Severity: LOW-MEDIUM

## Affected Products
- macOS

## Duration
- Ongoing

## Description
Audio playing through Bluetooth headphones/speakers stutters or cuts out when switching applications to/from fullscreen mode.

## User Reports
- "Every time I go fullscreen, my AirPods audio cuts out for a second"

## Workarounds
- Avoid fullscreen mode
- Use wired headphones
- Use built-in speakers
