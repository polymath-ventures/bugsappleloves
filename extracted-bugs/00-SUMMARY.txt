## KEY INSIGHTS

1. **Input bugs are most reported** - Text selection and autocorrect alone account for 18+ mentions
2. **Connectivity features are unreliable** - AirDrop and Hotspot are flagship features that frequently fail
3. **Search is fundamentally broken** - Mail, Spotlight, and Finder search all have major issues
4. **Long-standing bugs** - Many bugs have existed for 5-17 years
5. **Both platforms affected** - Most bugs span iOS and macOS, suggesting systemic issues
6. **UX inconsistencies** - Several "bugs" are actually confusing design decisions
