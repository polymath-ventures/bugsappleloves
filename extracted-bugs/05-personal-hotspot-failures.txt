# Personal Hotspot Connection Failures

## Mentions: 5+ commenters
## Severity: HIGH

## Affected Products
- iOS
- macOS (connecting to hotspot)

## Duration
- Years (ongoing)

## Description
iPhone Personal Hotspot feature fails to work reliably:
- Mac can't see iPhone hotspot even when enabled
- Connection drops randomly
- Requires repeated toggling to connect
- "Connect to iPhone" option grayed out
- Hotspot turns itself off unexpectedly

## User Reports
- "I have to toggle hotspot 3-4 times before my Mac sees it"
- "The hotspot just disconnects randomly during video calls"
- "Sometimes I have to restart both devices to get it working"
- "The 'Instant Hotspot' feature almost never works instantly"

## Workarounds
- Toggle Personal Hotspot off and on multiple times
- Forget network and reconnect
- Use USB tethering instead
- Restart iPhone
- Sign out and back into iCloud on both devices

## Related Issues
- Cannot "forget" a personal hotspot network
- Hotspot reconnects automatically when you don't want it to
