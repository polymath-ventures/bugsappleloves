# Apple Watch Mac Unlock Stops Working Randomly

## Mentions: 1 commenter
## Severity: LOW-MEDIUM

## Affected Products
- macOS
- Apple Watch

## Duration
- Ongoing

## Description
The "Unlock with Apple Watch" feature randomly stops working despite being enabled:
- Watch is connected and nearby
- Feature is enabled in settings
- Mac just doesn't recognize the watch
- Have to type password instead

## User Reports
- "Apple Watch unlock works for a week, then stops for no reason"

## Workarounds
- Toggle the feature off and on
- Restart both devices
- Sign out and back into iCloud
- Use password/Touch ID instead
