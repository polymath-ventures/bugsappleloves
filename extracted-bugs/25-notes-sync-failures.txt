# Notes App Sync Failures

## Mentions: 1 commenter
## Severity: MEDIUM

## Affected Products
- iOS Notes
- macOS Notes

## Duration
- Ongoing

## Description
Changes made in Notes don't sync reliably between devices:
- Edits on one device don't appear on another
- Notes disappear entirely
- Sync conflicts handled poorly
- Old versions overwrite new edits

## User Reports
- "I wrote a note on my phone and it never appeared on my Mac"
- "A note I've had for years just vanished"

## Workarounds
- Force sync by opening Notes on both devices
- Use a third-party notes app
- Manually copy important notes

## Impact
Can result in lost work and important information.
