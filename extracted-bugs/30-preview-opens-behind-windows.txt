# Preview Opens Files Behind Other Windows

## Mentions: 1 commenter
## Severity: LOW

## Affected Products
- macOS Preview

## Duration
- Ongoing

## Description
When opening files in Preview, the window appears behind other applications:
- Have to click on Preview in Dock to bring it forward
- Happens with double-click file open
- Inconsistent - sometimes works, sometimes doesn't
- Other apps open in front correctly

## User Reports
- "I double-click a PDF and nothing seems to happen until I realize it opened behind"

## Workarounds
- Click Preview in Dock after opening
- Use "Hide Others" command
- Keep Preview visible in front
